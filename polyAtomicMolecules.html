Polyatomic Molecules

Important Facts:

*Blue highlighted are the more important notes

  • Forms between many (>2) ions from representative elements
  • You will need to memorize the names in the "important" table under List of Polyatomics
  • Both elements should make a neutral molecule

Polyatomic Formulas

  • Parenthesis are used when you have multiple of a polyatomic.
  • Write the ion/polyatomics needed, then balance the charges as described in The Kamine Way

Naming Formulas
